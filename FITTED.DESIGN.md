Version: 1.0

Product: Fitted

# 1. Design Philosophy

Fitted is not a wardrobe manager.

Fitted is a personal AI wardrobe companion.

Every interaction should make users feel:

* Understood
* Guided
* Confident
* Stylish
* Organized

The product should feel less like software and more like having a personal stylist available every day.

---

# 2. Brand Positioning

## Core Statement

Find Your Perfect Fit Every Day.

## Product Identity

Fitted combines:

* The intelligence of ChatGPT
* The habit loop of Duolingo
* The personalization of Spotify
* The elegance of Apple
* The polish of Linear

---

# 3. Design Principles

## AI First

Users should feel AI assistance throughout the experience.

Avoid:

* Generic dashboards
* Static data presentation

Prefer:

* Recommendations
* Guidance
* Explanations
* Insights

---

## Emotion Over Analytics

Avoid:

"45% wardrobe utilization"

Prefer:

"You haven't worn this jacket in 143 days."

---

## Recommendation First

Users open Fitted for one reason:

"What should I wear today?"

Daily Fit should always be prioritized.

---

## Calm Technology

Reduce cognitive load.

Avoid:

* Dense layouts
* Excessive controls
* Technical terminology

---

## Premium Simplicity

Every screen should feel intentional.

If a component does not add value, remove it.

---

# 12. Daily Fit

Most Important Screen

Daily Fit is the heart of Fitted.

---

Hero Section

Should occupy 40–60% of visible screen area.

---

Required Elements

* Greeting
* Today's Fit
* AI Explanation
* Weather Context
* Occasion Context

---

Example

Perfect for your product review meeting.

You haven't worn this blazer in 18 days and today's weather makes it an ideal choice.

Dashboard layouts.

---

Reference

# 14. Smart Fit

Recommendation Engine

Each recommendation must explain:

* Why
* For what occasion
* Why now
